About Our Platform

Revolutionizing water well planning with artificial intelligence

Our Mission

The AI Water Well Predictor is designed to help communities, engineers, and water resource managers make informed decisions about water well installation. By leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms, we provide accurate predictions for well suitability, depth, discharge rates, and water quality based on geographical and environmental factors.

How It Works

Our platform uses Random Forest machine learning models trained on comprehensive datasets including soil types, lithology, land use patterns, rainfall data, elevation, and other environmental factors. When you provide location coordinates and site-specific information, our AI models analyze the data and generate predictions for:

  • Well Suitability: Probability assessment of whether a location is suitable for water well installation
  • Predicted Depth: Estimated depth required to reach the water table
  • Discharge Rate: Expected water flow rate in liters per second
  • Water Quality Index: Predicted quality score for the water source
